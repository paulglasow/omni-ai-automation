## Welcome to OmniAI v4

Your host has set up OmniAI v4 — a system that connects 4 of the world's best AI models into one interface. As a collaborator, you get access to:

- **ChatGPT** (OpenAI) — creative writing, brainstorming, coding
- **Claude** (Anthropic) — document analysis, long-form writing, reasoning
- **Gemini** (Google) — data analysis, research, math
- **Perplexity** — real-time web search and current information

You don't need to install anything. You just need the link your host sent you.

## Getting Started (5 minutes)

### Step 1 — Access the Interface

1. Open the link your host shared with you in any web browser
2. You'll see a chat interface with an AI model selector at the top
3. No account required — just start typing

### Step 2 — Choose Your AI Model

At the top of the interface, you'll see buttons for each AI:

| Button | Best for |
|---|---|
| 🤖 **Auto** | Default — OmniAI picks the best AI for your question |
| 💬 **ChatGPT** | Creative writing, coding, general questions |
| 🧠 **Claude** | Long documents, analysis, nuanced topics |
| ✨ **Gemini** | Data, math, research |
| 🔍 **Perplexity** | Current events, news, real-time info |
| 🌐 **All 4 AIs** | Get 4 perspectives at once (takes longer) |

> 💡 **Tip:** For most questions, leave it on **Auto** and OmniAI will route to the best model.

### Step 3 — Ask Your First Question

Just type in the chat box and press Enter. Try one of these:

- *"Explain how this system works in simple terms"*
- *"Help me write a professional email to [recipient] about [topic]"*
- *"I need to make a decision about [topic]. Help me think through the pros and cons"*

## How to Write Better Prompts

The quality of your question determines the quality of the answer. Use this formula:

```
[Context] + [What you want] + [How you want it]
```

### Examples

| ❌ Vague prompt | ✅ Better prompt |
|---|---|
| "Write something about marketing" | "Write a 200-word Instagram caption for a new coffee shop that targets young professionals, with a warm and aspirational tone" |
| "Help with code" | "I have a Python list of email addresses and I need to remove duplicates. Show me the simplest way with an explanation of each step" |
| "Research crypto" | "What are the top 3 arguments for and against Bitcoin as a long-term investment? Use sources from the past 6 months" |
| "Plan a trip" | "Plan a 7-day trip to Portugal for 2 people with a $4,000 budget. We love food, history, and nature but hate tourist crowds" |

## Common Use Cases

### For Work

**Writing tasks:**
```
Write a [email/report/proposal/summary] about [topic] for [audience].
Tone: [professional/friendly/technical]. Length: [short/medium/detailed].
Key points to include: [your bullet points]
```

**Research:**
```
I need to understand [topic] for [purpose].
Give me: a brief overview, the 3 most important things to know, 
and 2 things experts debate.
```

**Decision making:**
```
I need to decide between [option A] and [option B].
My situation: [brief context]
Factors I care about: [list]
Give me an honest analysis.
```

---

### For Creative Projects

**Brainstorming:**
```
I'm working on [project]. I'm stuck on [specific challenge].
Give me 10 ideas — from practical to wildly creative. No filtering.
```

**Feedback:**
```
Here's my [writing/design/plan/idea]: [paste it]
Give me honest, specific feedback. What's working? What's not? 
How would you improve it?
```

**Learning:**
```
I want to understand [concept]. Explain it:
1. In one simple sentence
2. With an everyday analogy  
3. With a concrete example
4. What's the most common misconception?
```

---

### For Personal Use

**Problem solving:**
```
I'm dealing with [situation]. I feel [how you feel].
I want [what you want to happen].
Help me think through this clearly.
```

**Planning:**
```
I want to [goal] by [timeframe].
Current situation: [where you are]
Create a realistic step-by-step plan.
```

## Tips for Collaborating with Your Host

### Sharing conversations

- Copy and paste interesting AI responses into your shared workspace
- Use the "All 4 AIs" mode for important decisions and share all 4 perspectives
- Screenshot particularly useful responses

### Working on shared projects

- Agree on which AI model to use for consistency
- Use the same prompts for comparable results
- Start each session by giving OmniAI context: *"I'm continuing a project about X. Here's where we are: [brief summary]"*

### Dividing work

| Collaborator | Suggested tasks |
|---|---|
| Person 1 | Research and information gathering (Perplexity) |
| Person 2 | Writing and drafting (ChatGPT/Claude) |
| Both | Review and refinement together |

## What to Do If Something Doesn't Work

### "The AI gave a wrong answer"

AI models sometimes make mistakes. Always verify important facts:
- Ask Perplexity for sources: *"Give me sources for [claim] with links"*
- Cross-check with another AI: *"Claude, fact-check this: [paste response]"*
- Use your own judgment for anything consequential

### "The response was too long / too short"

Tell the AI directly:
- *"Give me a much shorter version — 3 bullet points only"*
- *"Expand on [specific point] in more detail"*

### "The tone isn't right"

Specify the tone:
- *"Rewrite this in a more casual/formal/friendly/professional tone"*
- *"Make it sound less corporate and more human"*

### "It doesn't understand what I'm asking"

Try rephrasing:
- Give more context about WHY you're asking
- Break a complex question into simpler parts
- Give an example of what you want: *"Like this but for [my situation]"*

### "The interface isn't loading"

1. Refresh the page
2. Check your internet connection
3. Try a different browser
4. Contact your host if the problem persists

## Privacy & What You Should Know

- **Your conversations**: Stored securely in the system database. Your host can see conversation logs.
- **Your data**: Never used to train AI models
- **Sensitive information**: Don't share passwords, social security numbers, or financial account details in chat
- **AI limitations**: These are powerful tools but not infallible. They can be wrong, biased, or outdated.

**Q: Is it free to use?**  
A: Your host covers the costs. Check with them about any usage limits.

**Q: Can the AI remember our previous conversations?**  
A: Each session starts fresh unless you copy context from a previous conversation into the new one.
